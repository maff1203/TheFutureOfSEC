    What is PAULAT?

    Paul Atkins Token (PAULAT) is an innovative cryptocurrency designed to revolutionize decentralized finance. With a mix of humor and serious functionality, it aims to bridge the gap between utility and entertainment in the crypto world.

    REWARD

    Reward Rate: Users who provide liquidity to the PAULAT market receive a reward based on the amount of liquidity they add. The reward rate for liquidity providers is set at 2.5% (or 250 basis points).

    Criteria: Users must provide liquidity to the PAULAT trading pair on a decentralized trading platform like PancakeSwap.

    Reward Calculation: Rewards are calculated based on the amount of liquidity each provider has added to the liquidity pool.

    Volume Rewards:

    • Criteria: Users who generate high trading volumes by buying or selling PAULAT can receive additional rewards. These rewards are calculated based on each users daily trading volume.
    • Reward Cap: Users can receive up to a maximum amount per day based on the volume. For example, the maximum could be set at $25 per day for each user, depending on the amount of PAULAT traded.
    • Reward Calculation: Rewards are calculated as a percentage of the trading volume. If the volume exceeds a certain threshold, the reward will be proportional to the amount traded.

    Swap Rewards:

    • Criteria: Users who perform PAULAT swaps (e.g., using PancakeSwap) may also receive rewards. A 1% tax is applied on swaps, with half allocated to liquidity and the other half to user rewards.
    • Reward Distribution: 50% of the swap taxes are allocated to the liquidity pool, and 50% to the reward pool, where they are then redistributed to users.

    Reward Cap: There may be a cap on the total amount of daily or monthly rewards in order to limit the impact on token inflation and ensure that the rewards are sustainable in the long term.

    Rewards in PAULAT:

    • Distribution: Rewards are distributed in PAULAT to each eligible user. Users can claim their rewards through a claim function in the token contract or via an automated process.
    • Reward Calculation per User: The exact reward amount will depend on the amount of liquidity provided, the trading volume generated, and the swaps made.

    Participation Incentive Rewards (Staking Rewards): Users can be rewarded for their engagement in the project, such as participation in staking campaigns or loyalty programs.

    Burn and Rewards: A portion of the tokens is burned in each transaction to reduce the circulating supply. This creates upward pressure on the price of PAULAT, which can indirectly increase the value of rewards earned by users.

    Tokenomic

    1. Total Supply:

    • Total supply: 21 million tokens.

    2. Token Distribution:

    • Liquidity tokens: 3,000,000 PAULAT.
    • Reward pool tokens: 3,000,000 PAULAT.
    • Marketing tokens: 100,000 PAULAT allocated to the marketing wallet.

    3. Burn System:

    • Total burn amount: 14,000,000 PAULAT.
    • Daily burn: 350,000 PAULAT.
    • Duration: 40 days starting 1 day after launch.

    4. Rewards:

    • For liquidity providers:
      • Reward rate: 2.5% (250 basis points) based on liquidity added.
      • Daily cap: Up to $25 per user in PAULAT.
    • For trading volume:
      • Reward rate: 2.5% of daily trading volume.
      • Daily cap: $25 per user in PAULAT.
    • For swaps:
      • Tax: 1% on swaps.
      • Allocation: 50% to liquidity pool, 50% to reward pool.

    5. Caps and Limitations:

    • Daily reward cap: $25 per user per day.
    • Rewards limited to the available reward pool tokens.

    6. Automation and Distribution:

    • Automatic daily distribution at 11:30 PM UTC.
    • Manual claim option via the claimRewards function.

    7. Swap Taxes:

    • Tax: 1% applied to every swap.
    • Allocation: 50% to liquidity pool, 50% to reward pool.

    8. Liquidity and Control:

    • Liquidity and reward pools locked from launch.
    • Ownership renunciation scheduled after 1 day.
